## ğŸ“± Available Commands

### SMS Commands
- `MORE [topic]`: Get detailed information
- `ANALYZE [situation]`: Get AI legal analysis
- `LAWYER`: Request legal consultation
- `HELP`: Show available commands
- `STATUS`: Check compliance status

### Voice Commands
- Call our number to start a voice session
- Speak your legal question after the beep
- Press 1 to connect with a lawyer
- Press 2 to end the call
- Press # to finish recording
